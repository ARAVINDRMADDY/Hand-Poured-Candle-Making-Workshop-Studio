Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior candle-making experience?
Absolutely not! Our workshops are designed for beginners and seasoned crafters alike. Our expert instructors will guide you step-by-step through the entire melting, blending, and pouring process.
Can I take my candle home the same day?
Candles require about 45-60 minutes to set before they can be safely moved. During this time, you can relax, enjoy your drinks, or explore our retail section. Once set, you take it home in a protective box, though we recommend letting it cure for 2 weeks before burning.
What is your cancellation and refund policy?
We offer full refunds for cancellations made at least 48 hours prior to your scheduled workshop. Cancellations made within 48 hours can be rescheduled to a future date, but are non-refundable to account for prepared materials.
Are children allowed in the workshops?
Because we work with hot wax and concentrated oils, standard workshops are restricted to guests ages 12 and up (minors must be accompanied by an adult). However, we occasionally host special family-friendly events using safer, pre-cooled wax kits.
