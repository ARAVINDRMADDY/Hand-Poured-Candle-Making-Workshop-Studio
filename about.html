About Lumina Studio

Discover the passion, artisans, and expertise behind our premium candle making studio. We believe that true luxury lies in the perfect, intentional pour.

Our Story

How The Glow Started.

Founded over a decade ago, Lumina Studio was born from a simple realization: mass-produced candles were lacking soul, complex fragrances, and clean-burning ingredients. True home ambiance was missing.

What started as a small kitchen operation testing waxes and essential oils has now grown into a premier interactive workshop studio. We wanted to bridge the gap between high-end boutique candles and the joy of crafting something with your own hands.

Today, we've guided thousands of guests in discovering their signature scents, empowering them to light up their homes with pride and safe, sustainable materials.

Our Promise

The Sustainability Pledge

Beautiful ambiance shouldn't come at the cost of our environment. At Lumina Studio, every step of our supply chain and workshop process is vetted for ecological responsibility. We are committed to leaving a footprint as faint as the smoke from our clean-burning wicks.

  • Eco-Friendly Packaging

    All our workshop takeaway boxes and retail shipping materials are 100% recyclable, made from post-consumer waste, and use biodegradable packing peanuts.

  • Vessel Refill Program

    Bring your empty, clean Lumina jars back to the studio for a discounted refill pour. Reduce waste and keep your favorite containers glowing.

  • Carbon-Neutral Operations

    We partner with global reforestation projects, planting one tree for every 10 workshop tickets sold to offset our studio's carbon emissions.
