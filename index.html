The Methodology

How The Magic Happens

A meticulous four-step journey ensures every candle poured at Lumina Studio burns cleanly, brightly, and beautifully.

Step 01

Sourcing

We select only 100% natural, biodegradable soy wax and lead-free cotton wicks for a sustainable burn.

Step 02

Blending

Our artisans expertly combine top, middle, and base notes to create sophisticated, layered scent profiles.

Step 03

Pouring

Wax is heated to the precise temperature before being carefully hand-poured into prepared vessels.

Step 04

Curing

Candles rest and cure for two weeks, allowing the fragrance to fully bind with the wax for maximum throw.
